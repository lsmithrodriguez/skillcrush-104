Hi, my name is Lisa, and I have some advice I'd love to share!

Enjoying your food!

When I first started cooking, I LOVED butter and baking desserts! Cookies, breads, etc.

Basically, I enjoying eating and cooking but wasn't healthy about it. Side note: I also ran a lot, so I was fooled into thinking I was healthy.

Eating!!!!

When first starting out, it feels like there is a constant battle between watching what you eat and ENJOYING your food!

Cooking gives you a good start, you can CHOOSE how much butter or oil or to even hide cauliflower (substitute for rice! Smart!) in your meal.

That being said, a perfect time to spend some time thinking about your goals. Remember - ENJOY your food!
